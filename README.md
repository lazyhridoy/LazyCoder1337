### 📸 The Photographer Who Codes

> I didn't take the traditional computer science route. I'm a professional photographer who realized that a lot of the digital tools out there for creatives didn't fit our needs—so I started building my own. 
>
> I'm a big believer in **"vibe coding"**—jumping into the deep end, figuring out how things work on the fly, and writing code that solves actual, real-world problems.
